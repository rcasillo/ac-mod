# BMW M340i xDrive (G20)

## Overview
2019+ BMW M340i xDrive with B58 3.0L turbocharged inline-6 engine.

## Specifications
- **Engine:** B58 3.0L I6 Turbo
- **Power:** 382 HP @ 5,800-6,500 RPM
- **Torque:** 369 lb-ft @ 1,800-5,000 RPM
- **Drivetrain:** xDrive AWD (all-wheel drive)
- **Transmission:** 8-speed automatic
